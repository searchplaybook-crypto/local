# WMC Outcome-Aligned Investment Model v1.0

## Purpose

Create a fair commercial structure where WMC and the client share alignment around implementation quality and agreed optimisation outcomes.

The model recognises that digital optimisation is a process, not a single deployment event.

---

# 1. Digital Birth Certificate Investment

The Digital Birth Certificate is the foundational deployment.

It creates:

- verified digital identity
- structured business footprint
- semantic framework
- organic SERP optimisation foundation
- AI-readable business signals

Initial investment:

R7,000 standard deployment

---

# 2. Outcome Alignment Option

For approved clients, WMC may offer an outcome-aligned investment structure.

Structure:

35% of initial project value may be deferred as an optimisation success allocation.

Example:

Project value:
R7,000

Initial invoice adjustment:

Client pays:
R4,550

Deferred optimisation value:

R2,450

---

# 3. Target Agreement

Before optimisation begins:

WMC and client agree:

Current position:

- existing footprint
- visibility gaps
- digital risks

Target objectives:

- agreed improvements
- technical completion goals
- visibility milestones
- digital asset improvements

---

# 4. Optimisation Phase

The deferred portion represents continued optimisation value.

Activities may include:

- refinement
- testing
- iteration
- optimisation adjustments
- verification

The process:

Deploy

↓

Measure

↓

Improve

↓

Verify

---

# 5. Completion Recognition

The deferred amount becomes payable only when:

- agreed objectives are achieved
- evidence exists
- both WMC and client acknowledge completion

---

# 6. Commercial Protection

The model does not guarantee:

- search engine ranking positions
- specific traffic numbers
- algorithm outcomes

The model rewards:

- completed optimisation
- improved digital foundation
- measurable agreed outcomes

---

# 7. Purpose

This structure allows:

Client:
Lower initial barrier and shared confidence.

WMC:
Recognition for creating measurable digital improvement.

Both parties:
Aligned incentives.

END
